Prawomat is a Polish online platform that uses AI to generate formal legal documents — complaints, appeals, lawsuits, and formal requests — based on user input. It serves people who need to communicate with government institutions or corporations but cannot afford legal representation.

Live at prawomat.com with free and paid tiers.
