## What I Do

I build things at the intersection of psychology, product, and technology. Twenty years in academic research taught me how to think about measurement and human behavior. The startup side taught me how to ship. I co-found companies, design psychometric systems, and build products where behavioral science meets engineering.

### Product Development

Most products fail not because they can't be built, but because no one asked the right questions before building. I take products from zero to production — assessment platforms, content systems, growth tools — starting with what we're measuring and whether it actually works, not just whether it ships.

### Psychometric Design

Most assessments in the wild don't measure what they claim to. I design and validate instruments that do — using Classical Test Theory, Item Response Theory, and adaptive testing. The work is technical (item analysis, reliability estimation, factor structure) but the goal is always practical.

### AI Integration

I build structured workflows where AI handles architecture, implementation, and code generation while I lead product direction. This isn't prompt engineering as a hobby — it's how I run products across multiple companies simultaneously.

### Growth Engineering

Traffic that depends on manual effort doesn't scale. I build systems — programmatic SEO, content automation, technical growth infrastructure — that compound organic traffic through code.
