## What I Do

I work at the intersection of cognitive science, product development, AI, and growth engineering. My background in psychology and psychometrics shapes how I think about measurement, validation, and human behavior in digital products.

### Product Development & Strategy

From early-stage ideation through deployment — I scope, specify, and drive product direction. I've taken products from concept to production across psychometric assessment platforms, content management systems, and growth tools.

### Psychometric Assessment Design

Designing and validating psychological assessments using CTT, IRT, and adaptive testing (CAT). I build instruments that measure what they claim to measure — with the statistical rigor to prove it.

### AI Workflow & Product Integration

I don't just use AI tools — I've developed structured workflows where AI serves as a technical co-founder, handling architecture, implementation, and code review while I lead product direction.

### Growth Engineering & SEO

Building systems that scale organic traffic through programmatic SEO, content automation, and technical growth infrastructure.
