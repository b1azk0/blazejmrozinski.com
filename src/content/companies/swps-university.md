SWPS University is where I've spent the last twenty years. I joined the Department of Psychological Research Methodology in 2006 and never fully left. I earned my MSc, PhD, and a postgraduate diploma in UX Design here. From 2010 to 2024, I directed the Experimental Psychology Laboratory.

I teach methodology, psychometrics, data analysis, and R programming. The courses are quantitative and applied — how to design studies that produce defensible conclusions, how to build measurement instruments that actually work, how to analyze data without fooling yourself. My research has been funded by Poland's National Science Centre (NCN) and the National Centre for Research and Development (NCBR).

SWPS is one of Poland's leading private universities, particularly strong in psychology and social sciences. The academic work grounds everything I do commercially. The rigor I bring to psychometric design at [Gyfted](/work/gyfted), the way I think about measurement at [Nerds.family](/work/nerds-family), the systematic approach to testing at [Digital Savages](/work/digital-savages) — it all traces back to this department.
