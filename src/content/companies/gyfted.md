Gyfted (operated by Human Exponent, Inc.) is a venture-funded C-corp building AI-powered psychometric tools for recruitment and talent development.

I lead the product and psychometric side — designing assessment batteries based on Classical Test Theory (CTT) and Item Response Theory (IRT), integrating psychometric models with machine learning algorithms, and overseeing the full product lifecycle from conceptualization to deployment.

Our platform combines validated psychological assessments with AI to automate and improve hiring decisions, moving beyond resume screening to evidence-based candidate evaluation.
